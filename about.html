CASA HOTEL

In CASABLANCA, in the Agdal shopping district, the Flower Town Hotel & Spa, a brand new hotel flagship, opens its doors. Dedicated not only to the business world but to all visitors to Rabat, the clean decor of the Flower Town hotel has been carefully crafted in this district whose name in Berber means "garden". Comfortable and calm in the heart of the capital, the 5-star Flower Town Hotel & Spa provides its customers with attentive staff who will answer all their questions. On 5 floors, 100 rooms and suites offer modern comfort embellished with many services appreciated by travelers: Wifi - Restaurant - Conference room - valet parking - Fitness area with: Swimming pool, Gym and Hammam
